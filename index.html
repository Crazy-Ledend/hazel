Questions

Frequently asked.

Is Hazelnut free to use? +
Yes — completely. All core features including economy, pets, games, fishing, mafia, stocks, and social commands are free with no hidden costs or pay-to-win mechanics.
How does the economy work? +
You earn credits through daily rewards (with streak bonuses), work minigames, and games. Credits can be stored in a vault for safety, spent in the shop, used to buy stocks, or traded with other players.
How do I get pets? +
Redeem tokens earned through gameplay using the /redeem command. Once you have a pet, feed and play with it regularly — its hunger and happiness stats affect how it grows and whether it can evolve.
How does the Mafia game work? +
Start a lobby with ;mafia start, let 4–12 players join, and the bot assigns secret roles. Day phases involve discussion and voting; night phases allow role abilities. The game ends when one faction eliminates the other.
Can my data be used across servers? +
Yes. Your Hazelnut profile — economy, pets, inventory, fishing progress, levels — is tied to your Discord account, not any specific server. Play from anywhere.
